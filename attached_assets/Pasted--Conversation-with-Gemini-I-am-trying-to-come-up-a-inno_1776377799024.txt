1. The "Dual-Core" Strategy
To disrupt PitchBook and CB Insights, you must solve the "Tab Fatigue" problem. Analysts hate switching between a research tool and their CRM (Salesforce, Affinity, etc.).

A. The Standalone Engine: "The Capability Map"

The Logic: If a user searches for "Generative AI in Logistics," the engine doesn't just list firms; it maps the Capability Maturity of the sector. It identifies who has the talent (People), who has the patented workflows (Process), and who has the infrastructure (Technology).

Workflow Triggering: If a tracked company hits a specific "Capability Milestone" (e.g., hiring their first Head of Regulatory Affairs), your app automatically creates a "High Intent" task in the analyst's CRM.
